Our Story

Three Generations of Passion

✦

La Bella Notte was founded in 1998 by Lucia and Enzo Ricci, who left their village in Emilia-Romagna with nothing but a suitcase and a binder full of family recipes.

Today, their grandson Marco helms the kitchen — trained at the Culinary Institute of Bologna, refined in Michelin-starred restaurants across Italy, and brought home to honor the food that raised him.

We believe dinner should slow you down. Pull up a chair. Pour the wine. The pasta will be ready soon.
