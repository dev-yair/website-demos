Our Story

Born on the Street,
Built on Flavor.

Fuego Street Kitchen started in 2016 with one truck, two cooks, and a single recipe our grandmother swore would change lives. Turns out she was right.

We source locally whenever possible — our produce comes from farms within 50 miles, and our tortillas are pressed fresh every morning before we hit the road.

No shortcuts. No freezer bags. Just real food, made fast, served hot.
