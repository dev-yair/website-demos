// About Us

We're Detail Obsessed.

Apex was founded by Marcus Webb, a former automotive paint technician with 15 years in the industry. After seeing too many cars come out of detail shops worse than they went in, he decided to build something different.

We're not a car wash with a "detail" upsell. We're a dedicated detailing studio where every technician is trained in-house and held to a strict quality standard before they ever touch a client's vehicle.

Sedans, supercars, daily drivers, exotics — we treat every single one with the same level of obsessive care.
